GATE 2016

Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE 2016) is a National level test conducted for testing the aptitude and core subject knowledge of the candidates in the subjects of Engineering,  Architecture and PG subjects of Applied sciences etc. The candidates studying in the final year or have passed Engineering or Master of Science courses are eligible to sit in the GATE 2016 examination conducted by the joint committee of IIT’s and IISc. The GATE score is used for admission to PG programs in the field of Engineering (i.e. M.E/M.Tech and direct PhD) in IIT’s, IISc and various other government and private institutions located across the country. The GATE score is also used by various private and Public sector (PSU) companies for screening candidates for employment purposes.

 

Overview of GATE 2016

The GATE 2016 will be jointly administered by the IISc and seven IITs namely Mumbai, Delhi, Kanpur, Roorkee, Madras, Kharagpur, Guwahati.  The GATE organizing institute will be responsible for end to end process and making coordination among the other administering institutes. The candidates who qualify the GATE exam and pursue the Masters programs and direct doctoral programs in Engineering, Technology, Architecture and Doctoral programs will be given financial assistance by MHRD, government of India.

GATE 2016 Application Form

The application for the GATE 2016 will be accepted  through online mode only; the candidates will have to fill the application forms by accessing the zonal GATE websites of IISc or any of the other seven IIT’s. The candidates will have to upload their signatures, photographs, category certificate, and all other necessary certificates like degree certificate must be uploaded as mentioned in the GATE 2016 notification.

GATE Eligibility Criteria

The candidates appearing for the GATE 2016 exam must fulfill the eligibility criteria before appearing for the exam, the details of the eligibility criteria have been mentioned below:

-        The candidate should have completed B.E/B.Tech/B.Arch/B.Pharm from a recognized university.

-        The candidates should have completed master’s degree in any branch of Science/Mathematics/Statistics/Computer or equivalent.

-        The candidate having Bachelor’s degree in science (B.Sc research/B.S post diploma or 4 years after 12th) are also eligible to apply.

-        The candidates should have completed integrated M.Sc or five year integrated B.S. – M.S program.

The candidates who have passed the above listed qualifying examination, will have to submit their degree certificates or provisional certificates of the completion of the course.

The candidates who have completed integrated M.E/M.Tech after B.Sc  are also eligible to apply for GATE 2016.

All the candidates studying in the final year of the qualifying examination are also eligible to apply for GATE 2016, provided that they have to submit the certificate from the Principal of the college or university.

There is no restriction in GATE on the number of attempts a candidate can make.

GATE Exam 2016 Key Features

The GATE 2016 will be held online through a Computer Based Test (CBT), there will be 22 papers in the GATE 2016 examinations, and the candidate has to apply for one paper out of 22 as per the subjects in qualifying examinations.

The entire process of GATE 2016 will be online, i.e. starting from the filling of application form online, uploading of all necessary certificates and documents, download of admit card and final conduction of examination through CBT.

The candidates have to submit the application fees through online mode, by using different channels of fee payment as per the convenience of the students; these include Net Banking, Debit/Credit card, and e-Challan.

GATE 2016 examinations will be held from last week of January 2016 to 3rd week of February 2016, during forenoon and afternoon sessions on Saturday and Sundays of the alternative weekends. The complete examination schedule will be notified to the candidates as soon as any official information will be available.

 

The GATE admit card and result will be announced in the GATE 2016 official website;  candidates must note that no hard copy of admit card and score card will be sent to the candidates.

How to Apply

The candidates will have to apply online through an online interface known as GATE Online Application Processing System (GOAPS). The GOAPS interface will be used throughout the GATE exam 2016 and will help students in the following:

·        Applying for the examination online.

·        Uploading Photographs, signature and other necessary documents

·        Application fees payment through net banking, credit/debit card, e-challan.

·        Checking the status of application

·        Contact to the zonal GATE offices for any queries.

·        Checking Admit card and scorecard of the candidates.

The candidates will  have to access the GOAPS website from the website of the GATE zonal offices and also from GATE 2016 organizing institute.

The candidates will need to register by entering their details and valid mail id and phone number, since all the communication will be made through mail and phone no.

After successful registration,  GOAPS enrollment ID will be sent to the candidates to their mail IDs, The candidates must remember the enrollment ID and GOAPS password for further references.

Now the candidates will  have to apply online for GATE 2016 using GOAPS enrollment ID and password, the candidates have to enter the following details:

-        Personal information of the candidates that includes their name, date of birth, your personal mobile number, parent’s name and mobile no.

-        Address of the student

-        Eligibility qualification

-        Good quality photographs and signatures as per the specifications.

-        Scanned copies of all the mandatory documents.

-        Detail of the valid ID proof that the candidates have to take to the examination hall.

The candidates are advised to check all the details carefully and edit them before proceeding to fees payment.

After verifying all the details, the candidates can pay the fees  through net banking, e-challan or  credit/debit card.

After successful payment of the fees, the candidate will be redirected to the confirmation page, candidate may wish to take the printout of the final confirmation page for future references.

GATE Exam Pattern

The candidates, who are appearing for the GATE exam 2016, must check the GATE exam pattern to know about the number of  questions, marking schemes, important sections in the GATE 2015 question paper.

The question paper will comprise of total of 65 questions, for the total of 100 marks.  Out of 65 questions 55 question are based on the core subjects and engineering mathematics carrying 85 marks and the rest 10 questions will be from general aptitude which is a compulsory section carrying 15 marks.

The GATE question paper will comprise of two types of question as mentioned below:

1.      Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ)

The multiple choice questions will carry 1 or 2 marks, these questions will have 4 alternatives for every question, and the candidates will have to answer only one correct response.

2.      Numerical Answer Questions

They will carry 1 or 2 marks in each section of the question papers, the answer of these  questions  will have to be entered by the candidate by using the virtual keypad.  There will be no choices available for these questions.

GATE Admit Card

The candidates who have applied for the GATE 2016 exam, can check their admit card from the official website of GATE 2016. The admit card for the GATE 2016 examinations will be released online from 2nd week of December, 2016. The candidates have to login to the GOAPS by using their enrollment ID and email address.

The GATE admit card will contain the details like student roll no., name, date, centre of examination and examination schedule along with other necessary instructions for the candidates appearing in the GATE 2016 exam.

The candidates have to bring the GATE Admit card along with the valid Photo ID proof to get entry to the examination hall.

GATE Result

The GATE result 2016 will be announced in the official website of GATE 2016, candidates can check their result in the 2nd week of March, 2016. The candidates must note that the exact result declaration date hasn’t been declared by the authorities as yet.

GATE Score Card

The Scorecard for the GATE 2016 examination can be downloaded within a few days of the declaration of the result. The candidates can download the GATE scorecard by accessing the zonal websites from where they have applied for the GATE exam, the scorecard can be downloaded by entering the enrollment Id and password of the candidates. The GATE Score Card is valid for 3 years.
