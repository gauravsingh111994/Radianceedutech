ABOUT US

RADIANCE EDUTECH is an IIT-alumni venture conceptualized, conceived and created with the aim of providing cutting-edge technical education to the budding engineers of the country, with extensive hands-on, application-oriented training, backed by our years of corporate R&D experience with some of the best Fortune 500 Companies in the world. This is aptly complemented by our faculties drawn from the best of the IITs, to let the students enjoy and enhance their learning experience.

RADIANCE EDUTECH operations  include:

-      Summer Training Programs & Projects as part of B.E./B.Tech/MCA/M.Tech curriculum.

-      Specialized Coaching for GATE & IES.

-      Industry-Centric Skill-based Internship Training Programs

-      Technical Workshops & Seminars.

-      Expert Coaching for ECE/ICE/EEE/CSE/Mechatronics subjects.

-      School-Level Summer Training Programs & Workshops

These programs are conducted in the following domains:

-      Embedded Systems & Robotics

-      Analog Electronics Circuit Design

-      Communication Systems

-      Mechatronics

-      Power Electronics

-      VLSI

-      JAVA

-      PHP

We continually rework and revise our course contents, and augment in-house Lab facilities to  create avenues to train and equip our students for actual work situation with the industry. Our real-time projects ensure that the students have a high level of proficiency in their chosen core areas, and are job-ready once they step into the corporate world. Our Placement Cell Experts are actively working towards providing job assistance to all  the students registered with us for long-term/Summer Training Programs.

Our Scholarship Schemes and Free-ships for economically-challenged, meritorious students have been the talk of the fraternity at large, since its very   inception.

Besides, we also guide and mentor the under-graduate students on further prospects for their higher education aspirations and scopes of niche research in their chosen subjects.

We at RADIANCE INFOTECH thus aim and claim to do things differently, very differently.   The journey has already begun…
