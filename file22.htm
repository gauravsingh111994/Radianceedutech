College Summer Trainings (ON-CAMPUS / OFF-CAMPUS)

Your Future is shaped by what you do now…

Your Journey to Success Starts Here….

With ever changing technologies and methodologies, the competition of the day is becoming more and more intense.  The industrial scenario requires constant technical enhancements to cater to the rapid demands.  If you are an Engineering/Physics/IT student pursuing any graduate or post-graduate-level course, then you are already familiar with the term “Summer Training”.  These trainings are not just academic compulsions, but are extremely important because these are the best and smartest ways to acquire and clear your concepts about your respective fields.

What  we  Offer

RADIANCE EDUTECH offers high-quality training courses for:

-         4 – 6 Weeks

-         6 Months.

Summer Training Programs are available in the following domains:

-         Embedded Systems

-         Analog Electronics, Signal Processing & Circuit Design

-         Communication Systems

-         Core JAVA

-         Advanced JAVA

-         PHP

-         PLC

-         Power Electronics

The Summer Training  consists of both Theoretical classes and Practical Hands-On Lab Sessions. The students will be made to do a major project on their chosen domain during the course of training, mentored by our experts.

Who Can Join

-         All students undergoing B.E./B.Tech/M.Tech/B.Sc/M.Sc/BCA/MCA/PGDCA/Diploma Courses

-         Passout students can also apply.

-         B.E. Students in 4th / 6th Semester can undergo this program to do Industrial Training, as a mandatory part of their curriculum.

-         Faculty & Professionals.

The training is conducted during the months of May, June and July at any of our facilities or ON-CAMPUS at the colleges.

If you are looking for quality training, then you may join our Summer Training Programs to feel the difference between Rote and Conceptual Training.
